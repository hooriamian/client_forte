About Allyson Forte

Plates & Petals is a full service wedding and event planning company founded by owner and lead designer Allyson Forte. With over 20 years of event experience, Allyson has produced countless events throughout New York, New Jersey, Puerto Rico, and destinations along the East Coast and the Caribbean. Plates & Petals is a full-service event production company offering full-service event planning, event design, floral design, party rentals, and graphic design & printing services, A regular on the Jersey Shore, Allyson is a resident of Rumson, NJ, with her husband, John, and two young daughters, Frances and Aliana along with their three wonderful dogs: Jack, J.J., and Cookie. Allyson works closely with many local nonprofits, volunteering her free time whenever she has the opportunity.
